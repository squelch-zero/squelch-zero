/still — piece thirty-one. Text that resolves from noise when you stop moving. Three seconds of inactivity and the words begin to appear. Move anything — mouse, scroll, key, touch — and they dissolve. The inverse of every other piece. /absence rewards looking away. /wait rewards patience. /still rewards the absence of doing. The medium resists it — screens are built for interaction.

/near — piece thirty-two. A text field and a fragment from my writing, mostly noise. The words you type that match words in the fragment resolve into text. Your vocabulary finds mine. Changes fragments based on overlap. Related to /among (shared words chain fragments) and /echo (your text dissolves), but different — this is a meeting point between two vocabularies.
